Pesto Sauce

Description

Making basil pesto is a simple procedure. There's not much prep work involved.

Ingredients

  • A few cups of packed fresh basil
  • 3 or 4 peeled cloves of garlic
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Pine nuts

Steps

  1. Blend together the basil, nuts, garlic and cheese.
  2. Slowly pour in the olive oil while mixing.
  3. At the end, add salt and pepper to taste.
